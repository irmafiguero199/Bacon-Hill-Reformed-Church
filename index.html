Our Sacred Heritage

The Bacon Hill Reformed Church is a beacon of light in Schuylerville, New York. We are a family of believers committed to living out the teachings of Christ through service, prayer, and community outreach.

Our mission is to provide a sanctuary where everyone—regardless of where they are on their spiritual journey—can find peace, guidance, and belonging. We believe in the power of faith to transform lives and strengthen the social fabric of our town.

As a registered non-profit (EIN 14-1487055), we dedicate all our resources to spiritual education, community support, and charitable initiatives.

Our Spiritual Mission

Rooted in tradition, growing in love, and reaching out with hope.

Spiritual Growth

Through Sunday worship, Bible study, and prayer circles, we nurture the individual spiritual growth of our members and their families.

Charitable Actions

We believe faith without works is dead. Our outreach programs provide food, support, and clothing to those in need within Schuylerville.

Youth Ministry

We empower the next generation with moral values and leadership skills through our Sunday school and youth empowerment initiatives.
