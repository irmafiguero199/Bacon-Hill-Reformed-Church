Our Mission

"To grow in faith, share the message of Christ, and serve our neighbors with compassion and integrity."

Bacon Hill Reformed Church is a non-profit religious organization dedicated to providing spiritual guidance and community support in Schuylerville, NY. Our mission is to foster a welcoming environment where individuals and families can grow spiritually while engaging in meaningful service to those in need.

We are committed to the historical values of the Reformed Church, emphasizing education, community outreach, and social responsibility. Through our various programs, we strive to make a positive impact on the lives of our members and the broader community.
